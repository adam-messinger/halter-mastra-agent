# Farmer Questions Test Suite

Questions to test the Halter Farm Assistant's ability to handle real farmer queries.

## Overview

- How many cows do I have? How does that compare to last year?
- What mobs do I have?
- How many mobs?
- What are my biggest mobs?
- What is my stocking rate? Is that high or low?
- What's the average age of my cows? Is that too old/young?
- Is my herd good?
- What are my pet cows?
- What cows do I like the most?
- Which cows should I cull?
- Which cows should I dry off early?

## Health

- How many sick cows do I have?
- Do I have any sick cows at the moment?
- What 5 cows have been sick the most?
- What cows are sick the most often? What cows are often sick and also old or low breeding worth?
- How many cows have I lost this season? Is that good or bad?
- What are my best cows?

## Mating

- Is my mating season going well?
- How does it compare to last year, or how does it compare to my neighbors, or how does it compare to my neighbours vs how last year compared to my neighbours?
- How does it compare to industry averages (for system and farm type). What are my opportunities within mating?
- What cows/trends are good/bad?
- What's my cycling rate at the moment, how does it compare, is it good or bad?
- What's my non return rate at the moment, how does it compare, is it good or bad?
- What's my estimated in-calf rate at the moment, how does it compare, is it good or bad?
- Are my cows cycling well after calving?
- How many cows have I excluded from the season? Which cows?
- Have I intervened with many cows?
- How many of my cows did not cycle before the planned start of mating?
- What groups of cows are over performing or under performing for reproduction performance?
- What are the common ways to improve mating performance?
- Does the timing of AI and heat matter? Whens best?
- Did I miss any cows 'on heat' this morning?
